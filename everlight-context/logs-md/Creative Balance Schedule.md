- **Morning (9 AM - 12 PM):** Creative Writing (editing, polishing drafts, poetry)

- **Evening:** Planning for the upcoming week, restful downtime

---

### **Guidelines for Flexibility and Balance:**
- **Pomodoro Technique:** Use 25-minute focused sessions, then a 5-minute break to sustain productivity without burnout.
- **Flexibility:** Adjust activities depending on personal energy levels or inspiration; swap morning and afternoon if necessary.
