✅ **Fruit Base**  
- **Pineapple**: sweet-tart, juicy, bright acidity  
- **Strawberry**: sweet, mildly tart, smooth texture  
- **Blackberry**: deep, slightly earthy tartness  

Those three together = complex, layered berry-pineapple flavor that balances tart and sweet well.

✅ **Almond Milk**  
- Adds mild creaminess, light nutty note  
- Keeps it dairy-free, low-calorie  

✅ **Matcha**  
- Earthy, grassy, energizing (caffeine)  
- Pairs well with almond milk for a “latte-like” background  
- Bright green color  

✅ **Spirulina**  
- Intense green color  
- Slightly marine/earthy flavor  
- Nutrient-dense (protein, B12-ish analogs, minerals)  

✅ **Flavor Profile Notes**  
- Pineapple’s acidity helps cut the strong green/marine notes from matcha and spirulina.  
- Strawberries and blackberries mellow the edge of both matcha and spirulina while adding sweetness.  
- Almond milk gives it smoothness and subtle richness.  

✅ **Tips for Best Result**  
- Use ripe fruit or frozen fruit for better sweetness.  
- Add a little honey, agave, or banana if it’s too tart or green-tasting.  
- Start small with spirulina (¼ tsp) if you’re not used to its flavor.  
- Blend thoroughly so spirulina fully disperses.  

✅ **Nutrition Win**  
- Vitamins C and fiber from fruit  
- Antioxidants from berries and matcha  
- Chlorophyll and micronutrients from spirulina  
- Low fat and dairy-free
