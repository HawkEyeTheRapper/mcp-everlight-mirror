### What is an IMEI?
**IMEI (International Mobile Equipment Identity)** is a unique 15-digit number assigned to every mobile device with cellular capabilities. It identifies the device on a mobile network.

---

### What Can You Do With an IMEI?
You can:
- **Check if a device is blacklisted or reported stolen** using sites like:
  - [IMEI.info](https://www.imei.info/)
  - [Swappa IMEI Checker](https://swappa.com/esn)
  - [T-Mobile IMEI Status](https://www.t-mobile.com/resources/bring-your-own-phone)
- **Confirm compatibility** with a carrier
- **Track or block a stolen phone** through your carrier or device manufacturer (if you’re the registered owner)

---

### How to Use the IMEI Safely
If you're verifying a device before purchase or checking your own phone:
1. Go to one of the sites above
2. Paste the IMEI number
3. Review details like:
   - Make/model
   - Activation status
   - Blacklist status
   - Carrier lock status
