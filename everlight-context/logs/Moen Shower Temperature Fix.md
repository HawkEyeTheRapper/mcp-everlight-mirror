### **Most Likely Causes**
1. **Temperature Limit Stop Is Set Too Low**
   - Moen valves come with a plastic rotational limit stop to prevent scalding. If it's too restricted, it prevents the handle from rotating far enough toward “hot.”
   - **Symptoms**: Lukewarm water only, especially if other faucets get hotter.
   - **Fix**: Remove the handle and adjust the limit stop.

2. **Clogged or Partially Blocked Cartridge**
   - If debris or mineral buildup is restricting flow, especially on the hot side, it can both lower pressure and temperature.
   - **Symptoms**: Weak hot pressure, delayed heating.
   - **Fix**: Replace or clean the cartridge.

3. **Worn or Faulty Cartridge**
   - Over time, the cartridge can wear out or fail internally, which skews the mix.
   - **Symptoms**: Water never gets hot enough no matter what, low flow on hot only.
   - **Fix**: Replace the cartridge (usually a Moen 1222 for Posi-Temp).
